Infrastructure Intelligence Reimagined

TWINOVUS is a deep-tech infrastructure intelligence company redefining how assets are designed, delivered, monitored, and optimised across their lifecycle. We develop a modular, AI-driven Digital Twin ecosystem engineered for real-world deployment in both emerging and advanced markets.

The Problem

Infrastructure ecosystems suffer from systemic inefficiencies. While vast data is generated across design, construction, and operations, decision intelligence remains fragmented and reactive.

  • Disconnected lifecycle workflows between BIM, construction, and operations
  • Underutilised sensor data lacking integrated analytics
  • Reactive maintenance strategies increasing cost and safety risk
  • High-cost monolithic systems unsuitable for emerging markets
  • Limited predictive AI capabilities in current digital platforms
Infrastructure data exists — but lifecycle intelligence does not.

Our Solution

TWINOVUS delivers a scalable Digital Twin ecosystem that integrates data ingestion, analytics, simulation, and governance into a unified modular platform.

Core Capabilities

  • Unified Data Layer: BIM, IoT, inspections, GIS, and operational logs
  • Physics-Informed AI Models: Engineering-aware predictive systems
  • Real-Time Structural Health Monitoring
  • Predictive Maintenance Engine
  • Scenario Simulation & Risk Modelling
  • Explainable Governance Dashboards

Five-Tier Lifecycle Architecture

TWINOVUS is structured around a modular five-tier system enabling progressive adoption — from early design intelligence to autonomous infrastructure ecosystems.

Tier 1 — Design-Integrated Twin AI-driven optimisation and BIM-connected workflows.
Tier 2 — Construction Monitoring Twin IoT-enabled tracking and real-time progress analytics.
Tier 3 — Structural Health Monitoring Twin Real-time sensing and anomaly detection.
Tier 4 — Predictive Maintenance Engine AI-based degradation forecasting and cost optimisation.
Tier 5 — Autonomous Digital Twin Ecosystem Self-learning infrastructure intelligence systems.
